Risks

Development of the Mimi box as an idea was more complex than originally thought as considering the manor of implementation to basic components such as power source and code format where reconsidered through the process.

A primary concern of the entertainment device is the power consumption and therefore resulting game play time with our prototype this issue arose in which multiple options are viable but "All products covered by energy efficiency regulations must meet certain requirements before they can be supplied or sold in Australia or New Zealand.". This makes it so we must comply to a standard of modern efficiency prior to convenience which could dramatically impact the product output if prototype power consumption relative to final product power usage differ significantly.

Furthermore, the software as it is currently is recording inputs from raw Hz into .Wav files, which can be seen in the project testing section, is a great step in the correct direction. Beyond this, comparing the files and having a score-based system set around said comparisons to be the end goal as a functioning game mode which may be easier with Python, rather than java, the latter being the one the team has chosen for the prototyping phase which could cause minor disparagement in how code will function in the final product. It is a delicate risk due to the amount of variation the game itself must entail to be a global product that will need vigorous quality assurance for various dialects which could be problematic due to production time constraints, again leading toward an unfavourable unit quality on launch simply due to the large amount of variation that "differences between the speech of men and women, different social classes, and differences between age groups.

People will identify some of these features as marking the "best" or most "beautiful" form of the language, other features will be considered nonstandard or undesirable", a consideration that would have to be taken by region as the product is sound orientated and the inherent functional quality as well as sale output This would be riskily undertaken the less testing given a particular language or dialect variation and a considerable aspect to assess at a case by case basis as to reduce the impact on our Mimi's key trait of broad appeal and its most fragile asset.

In terms of hardware the casing would be majority of " Acrylonitrile butadiene styrene, more commonly known as ABS plastic. It is relatively inexpensive and lightweight, with a density of 1.08 grams per cubic centimetre" for sustained durability and cost efficiency which in itself brings a cooling risk based on ambient temperature and runtime. The manor of power implementation will vary the way in which we will have the batteries or power cable be connected and protected which we would require an airtight seal or case cover on the exterior to make the product more waterproof. Although, that would raise the risk for temperature and device handling limitations and should have terms and conditions to the situation where hardware or software insufficiency are in the fault of design, manufacture or user actions and would be assessed through testing. It may require fail safe measures like seen in the original Xbox 360's red ring of death which displayed the type of fault and severity of damage by the number and placement of red bars in the ring. Something like this for various functional issues would reduce risk to company image and allow us to offer repair or a supplementary service in return for product fault on our behalf, delicate implementation of insurance cover and user warranty to compensate for inevitable faults at any level of production or consumer use.
